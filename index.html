Islamic Wills App Value Proposition for Global Muslims

We are in the process of building UNIQUE, AI-BASED PRODUCT FEATURES that can create positive, innovative change in how Islamic Wills should be handled:

Main Point of Difference

Point of Difference

Focus on all types of both standard and custom wills, wills advice, and partnering with licensed Islamic wills estate lawyers to provide high level assurance to customers.

AI Agent Technology

AI Agent Technology

AI for questionnaires, chatbots/prompts, semi-automated wills compliance, and guidance for professional estate lawyers to improve every stage of the will's lifecycle.

Security

Security & Document Protection

Secure cloud document storage greatly reduces the risk of loss/damage that comes from only having physical will copies.

Source of Truth

Source of Truth & Version Control

Full audit trails and version history allow the platform to act as the definitive source for the will's content, supporting legal challenges and ensuring all changes are tracked.

AI Guidance

Review Reminders & AI Guidance

Automated reminders and AI suggestions help ensure ongoing will reviews with global estate lawyers, reducing the risk of disputes and making the process easier for users.

Collaboration

Collaborative Platform

Executors, Islamic charities/schools, beneficiaries, lawyers and accountants can all be invited to collaborate within the app, simplifying management and reducing misunderstandings.

Legal Review

Professional Review & Sign-off

Estate lawyers can review/sign-off on will creation and updates directly within the platform, to enhance enforceability.

Tax Integration

Tax Accountant Integration

Tax accountants may review and confirm current net assets positions as a free service, helping ensure accuracy and compliance.

Compliance

Death Event Compliance Center

Integrates authority letters, death event protocols and a secure key vault for asset/liability access, easing the executor's burden after the estate owner's death.

Dashboard

Live Dashboard & Visualization

Modern dashboard visualizes assets, liabilities, and planned distributions, offering a mind-map view for clarity and easy adjustment.

Community

Community Engagement

Ongoing engagement with live/recorded calls, demos, polls, and expert interviews with a move towards a paid community after the 18-month beta phase.

Marketplace

Marketplace Platform

Global, pay-as-you-go legal marketplace for will advice and services, built on revenue sharing models with law firms across jurisdictions.

Gamified Learning

Gamified Educational Module

A free, gamified learning platform for Islamic wills with user profiles, avatars, and a worldwide leaderboard to educate and engage users.
